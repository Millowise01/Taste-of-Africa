Couscous with Seven Vegetables

Couscous is a staple dish throughout North Africa. It consists of small steamed balls of crushed durum wheat semolina, traditionally served with a rich stew of meat and vegetables spooned over it.

Ingredients

  • 500g medium-grain couscous
  • 500g lamb or chicken pieces
  • 2 carrots, 2 turnips, 2 zucchinis, chopped
  • 1 can chickpeas, drained
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • Spices: 1 tsp each of turmeric, ginger, and cumin
  • 4 cups chicken or vegetable broth
  • 2 tbsp olive oil

Instructions

Show cooking steps
  1. In a large pot, brown the meat in olive oil. Add onions and spices, and cook for 5 minutes.
  2. Add the broth and hard vegetables (carrots, turnips). Bring to a boil and simmer for 30 minutes.
  3. Add the softer vegetables (zucchini) and chickpeas. Cook for another 20 minutes until all vegetables are tender.
  4. While the stew simmers, prepare the couscous according to package directions, typically by steaming it or adding boiling water.
  5. Serve the fluffy couscous on a large platter with the stew and meat spooned over the top.

Country of Origin

Couscous is a beloved dish with deep roots across the Maghreb region of North Africa.
