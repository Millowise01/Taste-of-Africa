North African Recipes

Discover the rich and aromatic dishes of North Africa.

Couscous

A staple dish in Morocco, Algeria, and Tunisia made with steamed semolina grains, vegetables, and sometimes meat.

Tagine

A slow-cooked stew from Morocco, cooked in a clay pot, often made with lamb, chicken, vegetables, and spices.

Harira Soup

A traditional Moroccan soup made with tomatoes, lentils, chickpeas, and spices, often enjoyed during Ramadan.
