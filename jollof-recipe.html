Jollof Rice

Jollof rice is a West African staple. It uses tomato, onion, and pepper.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups long-grain rice
  • 400g canned tomatoes or blended fresh tomatoes
  • 1 large onion, chopped
  • 1 scotch bonnet pepper, to taste
  • 1/4 cup vegetable oil
  • Salt and stock cube

Instructions

Show cooking steps
  1. Heat oil, fry onions until soft.
  2. Add blended tomatoes and cook until reduced.
  3. Add rice, stock, cover, simmer until cooked.
